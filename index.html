Privacy & Security

Your Keys Never Leave Your Browser

AetherIDE is 100% client-side. We don't have servers, databases, or analytics. Everything runs in your browser.

Local Storage Only

API keys, chat history, and settings are stored in your browser's localStorage. We never see or transmit your data.

No Backend Servers

Zero servers, zero databases, zero tracking. AetherIDE is a static web app — your browser talks directly to AI providers.

Direct API Communication

Your API keys go straight from your browser to OpenRouter, Google, or OpenAI. No middleman, no proxy, no interception.

Fully Auditable

Everything runs client-side in your browser. No hidden API calls, no telemetry, no background processes. What you see is what runs.
